Discover the Best Eats on Campus

Welcome to SpartanEats! As fellow Spartans, we know the importance of a great meal between classes. We've curated the best food spots within a half-mile of the Petty Building just for you. From mouthwatering international dishes to classic comfort foods, there's something for every palate. Join us as we explore the delicious flavors that make our campus truly special.
